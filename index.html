Is this program for your startup?

NUMA is looking for exceptionally talented and ambitious founders looking to build unique and impactful businesses :

  • Fast learning founders
  • Scalable business models
  • Unfair advantages
  • Technological leadership

What can you expect from this program?

In exchange for a 4.5% equity interest, NUMA provides each accelerated start-up with

  • Top Mentorship

    • Weekly office hours with our Entrepreneur-in-Residence and in-house designer
    • One-to-one workshops with 150 experienced entrepreneurs

  • Opportunities

    • Open access to NUMA's corporate partners
    • Qualified introductions to 100+ companies in NUMA's global client portfolio

  • Infrastructure

    • 6-month free office space
    • Access to NUMA’s foreign offices and partnering accelerators through the Global Accelerator Network

  • Financial Support

    • 3500€ bootstrapping cash
    • 18,000€ worth program benefits
    • 700k€ worth of perks from over 70 partners

Program Timeline

  • Washing Machine Clean up your ideas

    • Build a strong alignment between co-founders with "team building time"
    • Meet and connect with resources of the community during "Mentor Madness"
    • Validate milestones and metrics for the next 6 months
    • Working with a sense of urgency is core to the success of an early-stage start-up. The same applies to your time with us. The purpose of the first month is to expose you to experienced entrepreneurs and mentors to dive deep into your project, product and business model.

  • Run & GrowStructure a viable product and business

    • Move fast on hyper-growth oriented activities
    • Iterate with beta clients or clients
    • Build and perform a KPI oriented strategy
    • Sync with appropriate people in partners teams
    • Plan business development with new relevant metrics
    • The following 5 months are a high-pressure test and learn phase. In-house experts and successful peers will sit with you to make sure you execute successfully and don't sink weeks into non-core issues.

  • To Infinity & BeyondSharpen and share your vision

    • Structure a business plan
    • Build a collaboration path with partners
    • Pitch, pitch and pitch
    • Preparation of the post program
    • Demo Day with Journalists, VCs, Business Angels, Partners...
    • The objective of this final lane is to prepare the startups to fundraising and communication.
